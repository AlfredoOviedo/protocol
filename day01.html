Cultivating my body

One day, it occurred to me to set about cultivating my orchard for all I was worth. For my purpose, I used sun and steel. ~Yukio Mishima

Welcome to my blog. Here I will keep a log of my journey in cultivating my body.

Today I woke up with a need to distill the knowledge I’ve gathered about fitness and bring it into the physical world through my body. I see this as a creative endeavor — a way to capture what I know about gymnastikē: the education and moral cultivation of the body through exercise, training, and discipline.

Cheers.
